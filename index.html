About Me

Nathan Mosley currently studies at Miami University pursuing a double major in Games + Simulation and Creative Writing. He wants to go into writing for games or start up his own indie studio that focuses on telling interesting and unique stories. Fantasy is by far his favorite genre but he enjoys writing in all genres. He also enjoys writing in many styles from books to movies to poetry. What he cares about most is that the storytelling method is right for the story. Nathan has been passionate about playing games his whole life and grew up playing mostly Nintendo games especially Super Smash Bros. Most of his free time is currently spent playing video games (mostly visual novels), studying, and working on his own games.
